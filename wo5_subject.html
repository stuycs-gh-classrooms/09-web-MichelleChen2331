Iron Man(2008)


IMDB Page

About: After being held captive in an Afghan cave, billionaire engineer Tony Stark creates a unique weaponized suit of armor to fight evil.
The first Iron Man movie was nominated 73 times total and won 23 times. Tony Stark was portrayed by Robert Downey Jr.

Iron Man 2(2010)


IMDB Page

About: With the world now aware of his identity as Iron Man, Tony Stark must contend with both his declining health and a vengeful mad man with ties to his father's legacy.
The sequel was nominated 45 times and won 7 times. Robert Downey Jr. returns as Tony Stark with the introduction of Scarlett Johansson as Black Widow.
